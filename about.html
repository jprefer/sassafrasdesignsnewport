About Sassafras

Turning every house into a warm home, that's the essence of Sassafras Designs. We create coastal inspired spaces with a modern twist, allowing you to experience that fresh feeling again and again..... With experience designing homes from Newport, Rhode Island to Naples, Florida, Interior Designer Maggie Stansfield’s aesthetic combines a graceful sense of art and style as well as her extensive travel experience, while her well educated eye balances the perfect juxtaposition of lighting, color, texture and form to create dynamic spaces. Maggie embraces classic interiors with a coastal sensibility by using traditional elements and coastal motifs and infusing them with a modern aesthetic resulting in sophisticated, timeless, unique environments tailored to each client’s taste and style. Every project is approached with sensitivity to the architecture of each space utilizing scale and function to create highly effective and welcoming design solutions. How do you define your garden? At Sassafras, we also believe that your garden encompasses the whole of your landscape -- the planting beds, the lawns, and the spaces defined by them. We understand that a garden is a living, breathing, evolving space - our designs account for this and our fine gardening techniques honor it. Our mission is to develop, create, and maintain design vision and healthy plant communities and ecology in your garden. Our goal is to create gardens that truly inspire, blending timeless design with trending twists.
